Capstone Project: TerraCare

TerraCare is a soil regeneration consulting service that restores degraded farmland while promoting biodiversity and long-term profitability. The initiative aligns with UN SDG 15: Life on Land and integrates business viability with sustainability outcomes.

Under Macquarie University’s Work Integrated Learning framework, 60–80 students annually contribute to TerraCare’s development, combining academic research with real-world innovation. As the finance analyst, I developed a five-year investment model using NPV, ROI, and sensitivity analysis, achieving a projected ROI of 18.9% by Year 3, validating that ecological regeneration and profitability can coexist.

My work connected finance with marketing, accounting, and governance components. The marketing strategy refined TerraCare’s value proposition; the accounting chapter ensured transparent sustainability reporting; and the governance model implemented secure data practices aligned with ISO 27001 standards.

Insights from the Deputy Vice Chancellor of Macquarie University and the Chief Executive Officer of SoCreative guided the project’s strategic clarity and value definition, ensuring that TerraCare combined academic depth with commercial realism.
